Command list:
help
>this
scrub <optional number>
>goes through the last number of messages and deletes the bot's messages
emoji
>sends random emoji
joe
>sends 10 joe biden emojis
stop
>stops bot
say <words>
>sends words
roll <optional number>
>sends random number from 1 to optional number, default is 6
piclist
>sends list of pictures
pic <optional picture name>
>sends selected picture, no name sends random picture
pic add <name>
>adds picture to picture list
